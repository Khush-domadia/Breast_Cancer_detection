AI driven breast-cancer classification

The AI-powered Breast Cancer Classification initiative simplifies the process of checking for breast cancer online, much like a smart assistant. Picture of a patient on a user-friendly website where you can quickly and simply upload images of their mammograms. A unique computer program, like to a sophisticated brain, starts working behind the scenes as soon as you upload them.

This intelligent software has been trained to interpret mammography images with good accuracy. It carefully examines the photos that is crucial in determining the presence or absence of breast cancer. It provides with a result instantaneously after seeing the images. This result inform whether patient may have cancer or not.

Through the use of a straightforward website and clever technology, this project makes it simpler for everyone to maintain health.Furthermore, every amount of assistance in the fight against breast cancer adds up to greater results for all parties involved.
